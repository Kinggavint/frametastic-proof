From the first sketch to the final finish, every frame is a collaboration between you and our craftsman. Here's what we offer.

Custom Framing

Our signature service. Every custom frame begins with a one-on-one consultation where we discuss your piece, your space, and your vision. We hand-select the wood, design the profile, and build a frame that's perfectly suited to what it will hold.

Whether it's a family heirloom, a contemporary print, or a cherished photograph, we build frames that elevate the art and complement the room.

  • One-on-one design consultation
  • Hand-selected hardwoods (walnut, oak, maple, cherry)
  • Custom profiles and dimensions
  • Museum-quality glass options
  • Archival matting and backing

Wood Engravings

Our hand-engraved frames are where artisan craft truly shines. Using traditional carving tools and techniques, we engrave intricate designs directly into the frame — from mountain landscapes and botanical motifs to custom monograms and family crests.

Each engraving is unique, following the natural grain of the wood. No two are ever identical, making every piece a true original.

  • Hand-carved with traditional tools
  • Custom motif design (landscapes, botanicals, geometric)
  • Monogram and crest engraving
  • Natural grain integration
  • Oil or wax finish for depth

Metalwork

For a more contemporary or industrial aesthetic, our metalwork frames bring bold character to any space. We work with copper, brass, iron, and steel — hammering, bending, and finishing each piece by hand.

Our metal frames can stand alone as a statement piece or combine with wood elements for a mixed-media design that bridges rustic warmth with modern edge.

  • Copper, brass, iron, and steel options
  • Hand-hammered textures
  • Natural patina and oxidized finishes
  • Corner brackets and accent details
  • Wood-metal combination designs

Painting

Our painted frames add color, warmth, and personality. Using fine art techniques — washes, dry-brushing, layered glazes, and distressing — we create finishes that feel lived-in and intentional, never flat or factory-made.

Popular for watercolors, children's art, botanical prints, and pieces that call for a softer, more personal touch. Every painted frame is hand-applied and unique.

  • Custom color matching to your space
  • Wash, glaze, and distressed techniques
  • Gold-leaf and metallic accents
  • Whitewash and antiqued finishes
  • Sealed with archival-quality topcoat

Our Process

From Conversation to Creation

Every project follows the same thoughtful path — ensuring your frame is exactly what you envisioned.

1

Consult

We start with a conversation about your piece, your space, and your style. We'll discuss materials, dimensions, and design direction.

2

Design

We create a concept tailored to your vision — selecting wood species, finishes, and detailing that complement your artwork perfectly.

3

Craft

Hands-on building using traditional and modern techniques. The frame takes shape through careful cutting, joining, finishing, and detailing.

4

Deliver

Your finished frame is carefully packaged and delivered. We can also handle installation to make sure it looks perfect on your wall.
